MARRIOTT
Hotel Karachi

Across from Frere Hall and Park, the 5-star Karachi Marriott Hotel offers modern and luxurious rooms with a flat-screen cable TV. It features 3 dining options, spa and outdoor swimming pool. The air-conditioned and fully carpeted rooms each have their own private bathroom, which include free toiletries, shower, hairdryer and slippers. An electric kettle and minibar can be found in each room. Karachi Marriott Hotel is 302 m from the Hockey Club of Pakistan and less than half a kilometer from Holy Trinity Cathedral. National Museum of Pakistan is 2.4 km away. Karachi Jinnah International Airport is 14.5 km from the hotel. Guests have access to a 24-hour business center while those staying on the executive level can use a private lounge. In the hotel, guests will find a drugstore, florist and currency exchange. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available for an extra fee.
